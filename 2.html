DEBUT

2015: SM Rookies
In 2013, Doyoung became a trainee through SM Entertainment's Casting System after being scouted at a singing competition. In 2015, he was introduced as a member of SM's pre-debut training team, SM Rookies as he became an MC on MBC Music's Show Champion alongside fellow SM Rookie, Jaehyun. They hosted the program from January 21 to July 1, 2015.
